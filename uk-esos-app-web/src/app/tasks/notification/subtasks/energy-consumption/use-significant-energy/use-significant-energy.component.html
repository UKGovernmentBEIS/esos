Your organisation may choose to carry out an energy audit or use an alternative compliance route for areas of significant energy consumption. This means those assets and activities which together account for not less than 95% of the participant's total energy consumption.

The de minimis is a maximum of 5% of total energy consumption which may be excluded from the energy audit or alternative compliance route. The percentage figure for your organisation must be entered as 'Energy use not covered by any compliance routeâ€™ and be a whole number. You may round up a decimal to 5% where that is the nearest whole number, but you may not round down to 5%.

Energy can be excluded on the basis of one or a combination of these factors:

  • site
  • group
  • asset
  • activity
  • fuel used
