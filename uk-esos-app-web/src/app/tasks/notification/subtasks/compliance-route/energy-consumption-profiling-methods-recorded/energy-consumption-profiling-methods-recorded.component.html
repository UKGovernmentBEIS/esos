The evidence pack needs to contain:

  • Details of the methods used and the extent to which it was used.
  • The reasons why the energy audit does not include an analysis based on energy consumption profiles.

This would have been carried out by the Lead Assessor, who may be part of your organisation or external.

If you are unsure if this information is in the evidence pack, speak to your Lead Assessor.

ESOS evidence pack is an information pack that shows how the assessors carried out the assessment and audit. Ensure this has been given to you by your lead assessor.
