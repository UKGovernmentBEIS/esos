Energy consumption profiles are a breakdown of how energy is used, and how that energy use varies.

For example, in an office building, the energy consumption profile could include energy used for heating, ventilation, and appliances.

All significant energy consumption is covered by ISO 50001 certification, Display Energy Certificates or Green Deal Assessments

Energy consumption profiles are a breakdown of how energy is used by a particular asset or activity and how that energy use varies.

Your analysis of energy consumption of buildings, transport and industrial processes for your ESOS audit must be based on energy consumption profiles where appropriate and reasonably practicable.

Where this is not the case, you must give a reason in your evidence pack and refer to this in your compliance notification to your regulator.
