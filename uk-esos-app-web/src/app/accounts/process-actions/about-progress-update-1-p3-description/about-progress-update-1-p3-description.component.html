Participants in the third compliance period for ESOS are required to submit two progress updates. You will not be able to submit progress updates unless you have submitted an action plan (you will be asked to confirm this in MESOS).

Submitting progress update 1

You must complete the first of the two progress updates if you are:

  • A responsible undertaking which submitted an action plan for the third compliance period under an existing MESOS account.
  • An undertaking that has left its highest parent group / participant following submission of an action plan, which has not agreed to comply with its previous or new group so must comply on its own behalf as the responsible undertaking, under a new MESOS account.

You will be asked whether you have implemented any measures that were proposed in your action plan, and whether you have implemented any measures that were not included in your action plan. Estimates will be needed in relation to each implemented measure of the reduction in energy consumption resulting during the period between 6 December 2024 and 5 December 2025. On an optional basis, you will also be able to provide estimates for implemented measures of any reductions in energy consumption resulting during the period between 6 December 2023 and 5 December 2024. All progress updates will be published by the Environment Agency.Â 

Only the responsible undertaking can complete and submit progress updates in MESOS. It will not be possible for lead assessors to enter, edit or review the information.

You will be able to save the information entered for your progress updates at any time and return to complete it, before submitting it.
