You must read the Environment Agency's latest guidance on action plans in issue 30 of its October 2024 ESOS newsletter (which can be obtained by emailing esos@environment-agency.gov.uk) before starting the action plan. An action plan is required to be provided following submission of a notification of compliance (NOC) for the third compliance period (which had a compliance date of 5 June 2024, extended to 6 August 2024).

A template is provided to enable collection of information prior to entering it in MESOS: Action Plans template.xlsx

Submitting the action plan

You must complete an action plan if you are:

  • A responsible undertaking which submitted a NOC for the third compliance period under an existing MESOS account.
  • An undertaking that has left its highest parent group / participant following submission of the NOC, which has not agreed to comply with its previous or new group so must comply on its own behalf as the responsible undertaking.

When you start your action plan, you will be asked whether you have any energy efficiency measures proposed to be implemented by 5 December 2027; these can be measures from your ESOS assessment or another source, and include measures that relate to other schemes. If you answer 'no', your action plan will be a statement that you propose to implement no measures. If you answer 'yes', you will need to add your energy efficiency measure(s) and provide information about them. All action plans will be published by the Environment Agency.

Only the responsible undertaking can complete and submit an action plan in MESOS. It will not be possible for lead assessors to enter, edit or review the information.

You will be able to save information in your action plan at any time and return to complete it, before submitting it.
